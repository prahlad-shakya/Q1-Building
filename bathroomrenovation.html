COMBINED SHOWER AND TUB BATHROOM

A combined shower and tub is great space saving solution for small bathrooms, offering top-quality tiles and durable waterproofing. With options like ceramic tiles and sleek glass enclosures, it bends style and function. EasyRenovation designs and installs walk-in shower bathrooms in Mississauga, helping with material selection, delivery, and a smooth renovation.

  • Disconnecting water and electricity
  • Removing old tiles, sinks, toilets, and bathtubs
  • Wall and floor inspection
  • Repairing or reinforcing surfaces
  • Waste disposal and site preparation

WALK-IN SHOWER BATHROOM

Walk-in showers offer flexibility with fixtures like handheld showerheads, adjustable shower bars, and built-in seating for added comfort. Combined with non-slip ceramic tiles and durable waterproofing, it's smart choice for any bathroom. Easy Renovation handles installations and delivers all necessary materials for a flawless upgrade.

  • Site inspection and measurements
  • Budget estimation
  • Selecting design and style
  • Choosing tiles, fittings, vanities, and lighting
  • Creating design mockups or blueprints

MASTER BATHROOM

A master bathroom creates a personal spa with luxury features like dual showerheads, elegant tile designs, and large vanities. Paired with frameless glass enclosures and modern fixtures, it enhances both beauty and functionality. Easy Renovation specializes in custom master bathroom installations, providing premium results.

  • Plumbing and electrical work
  • Tiling walls and floors
  • Installing shower units, toilets, and sinks
  • Fitting lighting, mirrors, and storage units
  • Waterproofing and ventilation setup

POWDER ROOM

A powder room, also known as a guest bathroom or half-bath, is a small yet stylish space typically located near the main living area for guests' convenience. While compact in size, it offers the perfect opportunity to make a bold design statement. Often featuring a toilet and a sink, this space can be beautifully adorned with luxurious finishes, dramatic wallpaper, elegant lighting, and thoughtfully chosen fixtures to leave a lasting impression.

  • Paint touch-ups and caulking
  • Cleaning and polishing
  • Safety and leak checks
  • Client walkthrough and feedback
  • Final adjustments and handover
