Building Maintenance and Operations

This includes the day-to-day upkeep of a facility to ensure it remains safe, functional, and efficient. It involves both preventive and corrective maintenance of infrastructure, systems, and utilities.

  • HVAC, plumbing, and electrical system maintenance
  • Preventive maintenance schedules
  • Emergency repairs and response
  • Energy efficiency optimization
  • Vendor and contractor coordination

Space Planning and Utilization

Effective space management ensures that workspaces are optimally designed for productivity and safety. It includes planning layouts, managing occupancy, and adapting spaces to meet organizational needs.

  • Floor plan design and optimization
  • Workspace allocation and reconfiguration
  • Desk booking systems
  • Space utilization analysis
  • Compliance with health and safety regulations

Health, Safety, and Environmental Compliance

managers ensure the facility meets all health, safety, and environmental standards. This includes fire safety, sanitation, and sustainable practices.

  • Conducting risk assessments and safety audits
  • Managing waste disposal and recycling
  • Ensuring compliance with OSHA/local safety laws
  • Emergency planning and evacuation procedures
  • Promoting green initiatives

Asset and Resource Management

Facilities management includes tracking and maintaining all facility-related assets such as furniture, equipment, and technology, ensuring longevity and cost efficiency.

  • Asset inventory and tagging
  • Lifecycle management of equipment
  • Budgeting and cost control
  • Procurement and contract management
  • Software tools for facility asset tracking
