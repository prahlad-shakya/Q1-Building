Building Renovation

Revitalising Spaces with Purpose

We specialize in transforming outdated or damaged buildings into refreshed, functional, and modern spaces. Whether it's a residental property, commercial facility, or strata building, Q1 BUilding Services and Maintenance delivers renovation solutions tailored to your needs, timeline, and budget.

  •   We upgrade interiors and exteriors to enhance functionality, aesthetics, and value.
  •   We coordinate all trades and materials to ensure efficient, high-quality work.
  •   We focus on safety, compliance, and structural integrity throughout the renovation.
  •   We deliver renovations that reflet your vision while meeting today's building standards.
Modernised Spaces, Lasting Results

We breathe new life into old or worn structures--enhancing aesthetics, usability, and property value through thoughtful renovations.

Custom Renovation Solutions

Every building is unique. We tailor our renovation solutions to match your specific goals, from home updates to full-scale refurbishments.

Quality work with Minimal Disruption

Our team ensures efficient timelines, safe practices, and tidy sites-- delivering quality finishes while keeping your space functional.

Assessment and Planning

This stage involves inspecting the building to evaluate its current condition and define the renovation goals. Structural integrity, safety compliance, and design requirements are assessed. A detailed plan is created based on budget, timeline and desired improvements

  • Site and structural assessment
  • Identifying areas for repair or upgrade
  • Cost estimation and budgeting
  • Defining scope and timeline
  • Obtaining necessary permits and approvals

Demolition and site preparation

Outdated, damaged or unnecessary structures are safely demolished. This stage also includes clearing debris and prepping the space for reconstruction, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.

  • Removal of old materials, fixtures, or finishes
  • Hazardous material handling
  • Site cleaning and leveling
  • Temporary support structures
  • Safety precautions and signage

Structural and Interior Renovation

Core construction work is carried out, including structural repairs, wall modifications, flooring, and roofing. Interior updates such as electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems are also installed or upgraded.

  • Reinforcing or replacing structural elements
  • Electrical rewiring and plumbing upgrade
  • HVAC system installation
  • Flooring, ceiling, and wall work
  • Installation of windows, doors, and insulationp

Finishing and Handover

This final stage includes painting, lighting, furnishing, and cleaning. After all elements are completed, the building is inspected for quality, safety, and compliance. It’s then handed over for use.

  • Interior painting and decorative finishes
  • Lighting fixtures, cabinetry, and furniture
  • Deep cleaning and polishing
  • Final inspections and certifications
  • Client walkthrough and handover
