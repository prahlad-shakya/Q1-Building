About Us

Q1 Building Services

We're a tight-knit team of experts, tradespeople, and problem solvers. As a boutique building company, we offer top-notch, personalized service and love having our clients involved in their dream projects. We provide a range of trade services accross Queensland and NSW.


We team up with a qualified architect and building certifier to provide the best services around. If you don't have plans yet, we can help design your dream project and handle the Council approval. We take care of every detail from start to finish, guiding you every step of the way for a stress-free experience.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and a seamless construction experience. We are committed to growing our business while maintaining the personal approach that sets us apart from the rest.
